Neurological Physiotherapy or Neuro Rehabilitation

Neurological physiotherapy involves the assessment and treatment of conditions that affect the nerves of the brain or the body. People with neurological conditions can experience difficulties of physical function including problems with muscle strength, range of movement, co-ordination, sensation, balance and mobility.

Neurological rehabilitation utilises the principles of neuroscience and neuroplasticity, movement science and exercise therapy to guide treatment aimed at improving an individual’s physical function and independence.
